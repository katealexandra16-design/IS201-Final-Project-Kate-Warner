About the US Open

The US Open is one of the four Grand Slam tennis tournaments, held annually in New York. It features the worlds top players competing for one of the most prestigious titles in tennis. My family lives jsut outside New york and every year we venture into the city to watch the best players compete.

History

The US Open began in 1881 and has evolved into a global sporting event. It was the first Grand Slam to introduce night matches and tiebreaks. The Even was originally held in Rhode Isalnd but in 1915 it was moved to Forest Hills Stadium in Queens, NY. In 1978 the tournament moved to Arthur Ashe Stadium in Flushing Meadows, NY, it is still held here today.
